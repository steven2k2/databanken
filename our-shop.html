Our Shop

Databanken sells equipment on eBay that is not useful for low-income individuals or community groups. These items may be bulky, faulty, vintage, enterprise equipment, one of a kind, or end-of-life equipment. Listed laptops may have issues, marks, or scratches. Excess stock is also sold.

Our Shop

We offer affordable, refurbished computers to concession card holders, students, pensioners, and community groups.

All our computers are refurbished, quality assured, and come with a three-month back-to-base warranty. For desktops, the warranty extends to six months if the Linux operating system remains unchanged. All our computers include Linux and useful open-source software and come with a free user guide.

Desktop Computers

We usually have around 10 desktop computers in stock and ready to go at any time. You can view a live updated list via our stocklist.

Members of the public who do not hold a concession card may still purchase a desktop from us; however, prices are 50% higher (e.g., a $50 desktop would be $75). All our Linux-based computers are internet-ready, include word processing software, and more.

Laptop Computers

Laptops are only available to concession card holders. Pricing depends on specifications.

Some models include a bag and mouse; otherwise, these are available for a small price. Speak to a staff member to view available laptops when visiting our shop.
