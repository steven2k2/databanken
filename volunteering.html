Volunteering at Databanken

Databanken’s volunteers range from technical experts to hobbyists and come from many cultural and linguistic backgrounds.

Duties

  • Recycling electronic equipment
  • Supporting welfare and community-based organisations
  • Helping to provide access to computing for people on low incomes

We use open-source software on all our computers rather than Windows, as we are committed to free and open-source technology.

Volunteer Commitment

To be a volunteer at Databanken:

  • You must commit to at least one shift per fortnight.
  • You should be comfortable working in a team environment.
  • Volunteers are expected to log in regularly to sign up for shifts and check for updates.
  • Housekeeping is a shared responsibility to keep workspaces clean.

Ready to Apply?

You can apply right now via our Volunteer Application form. Tell us about your skills, and ideally, include your résumé. We will invite you to an information session where we can discuss volunteering. Information sessions are held regularly.

Volunteer Positions Available

Front Desk & Customer Service

A great way to gain IT retail experience!

Duties

  • Assist customers in-store and over the phone.
  • Explain Databanken low-cost Linux computers.
  • Help with administrative tasks.

Volunteer Requirements

  • Comfortable working with Linux desktops
  • Willing to learn Databanken’s custom Linux distribution.

Technical/Buildroom & Warehouse

We build and refurbish reliable computers following strict quality control.

Duties

  • Assemble and test refurbished computers.
  • Wipe & sanitise hard drives.
  • Recycle broken components properly.

Volunteer Requirements

  • Familiarity with GNOME, MATE, and Linux is essential.
  • Basic command-line skills (e.g., navigating files, checking system status).
  • Familiarity with hardware is a plus.

Other Ways to Help

Volunteers can also contribute to:

  • Sales Database Management
  • Website Development & Maintenance
  • Linux Distribution Customisation
  • Internal Network Management
