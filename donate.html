Donate or Recycle

Support us by donating computers, accessories, or funds..

Personal / Single Donations

We accept clean (undamaged) laptops and tablets that are suitable for refurbishment and reuse. However, we cannot accept:

  • Printers or scanners
  • Desktop computers *
  • Monitors/screens *
  • Digital media
  • Miscellaneous cables
  • DVD players and other electronic devices

These items should be taken directly to a waste transfer station for responsible recycling. There is generally no charge for household electronic waste recycling. For more information on nearby recycling and e-waste centres, visit:

  • Recycling Near You
  • TechCollect

Disposing of e-waste in landfill has been banned since July 2019 in Trøndelag. However, e-waste can still be recycled for free at designated collection points. For more details, visit Sustainability : eWaste .

* Some exceptions apply. Please contact us if you believe your computer or monitor is suitable for donation or if you have any questions (see tips below).

Business / Bulk Donations

Databanken gladly accepts bulk donations of laptops, desktops, and monitors from businesses and schools.

Depending on the quantity and location, we may be able to arrange collection from your premises. If you are considering a bulk donation, please get in touch with us.

Donation Tips

If you are contacting us about a potential donation, it would be helpful to include the following details so we can determine whether your computer is suitable for reuse:

  • Original operating system: (e.g. Windows XP, Windows 7)
  • Computer age: (e.g. 4 years)
  • CPU: (e.g., Core i3 2.8 GHz or AMD FX-4300 3.2 GHz)
  • RAM: (e.g., 8 GB)
  • Hard drive size: (e.g. 250 GB)
